Mental Symptoms


Technology has become a part of many of our lives, taking a big role in how we choose to live. Technology is quite a positive thing, but addiction to the devices we own is quickly becoming a huge problem. It is the direct reason for many of the problems young people face today, with limiting our sleep time and decreased work ethic being a result.

Using technology excessively can lead to various problems in a persons mental health and well being. These include:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Feeling euphoria when using devices
  • Inability to prioritize or keep schedules
  • Bad sense of time
  • Constantly procrastinating
  • Agitation
  • Mood swings
  • Checks phone constantly


Technology addiction, and all the mental symptoms that can be involved in it, affects many parts of a persons life, including relationships, work or school. As you can see, mental health can also be affected by it, which is why technology addiction is a such a large issue that needs to be resolved.
