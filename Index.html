What is Addiction to Technology?

All around the world, there are people affected by an issue so common, that no one thinks it's a problem. This is technology addiction. People of all ages, from children as young as 4, to adults who already have jobs and responsibilities are spending a large, unhealthy amount of time on their devices using things like social media, the internet, or playing games. While technology can be a very useful, or entertaining tool, using it too much can lead to several negative symptoms, both physical, and mental.
